SUMIT NIKHARE

UI/UX Designer - Web Developer

I'm a Gurugram-based UI & User Experience Designer who always aim to create high-quality products where functional, aesthetic and experience design work together in harmony. For me, UX is about solving problems for the users and generating positive emotions amongst them whilst doing so.

I have completed my engineering degree in Textile technology, while pursuing my degree I become familiar with graphics design. My interest really peaked when I came to know about Human-centered Design.

Within the design field, I can be defined as a generalist because I can assist in all stages of the product development life cycle - insight work, concept development, analysis, interaction design, prototyping, user interface, user testing and wireframes.
