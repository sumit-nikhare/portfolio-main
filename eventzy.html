Eventzy App UI/UX Casestudy

EVENTZY is an application to help user create and manage events and also add friend and have discussion about the event. Users can meet new people with similar interests and get to know them by participating in the event. User can share event memories with other friends and also add stories of the event.

My Role

Ideation, Research, Define, UI, Usability Testing & Documentation

Tools

Figma, Adobe Ai, Google Forms, Invision

Duration

6 weeks

Application

iOS, Android

Problem Statement

With the increasing busy life people don't get time for social interaction & to meet new people. Organising and managing event takes lot of time.
Also due to COVID - 19, people are not spending enough time with their friends and family.

Solution

An digital solution which lets user plan events with involving friends in the discussions. Organising social event to meet new people with similar interest. Also lets user share event photos, videos, stories with friends and family

User Research

User Research focuses on understanding user behaviour, needs, motivations through observation techniques, task analysis and other feedback methodologies. I have followed Qualitative Analysis. In Qualitative Analysis, I took interview of 8 people.

Questions

  • How old are you?
  • Are you currently working?
  • Do you particpate in social events?
  • Do you like meeting new people with similar interest?
  • How do you currently do it?
  • How do you currently plan your events?
  • How do you think COVID 19 has affect social events and gatherings?
  • Do you use any application or service for event organisation and management?

Insights

The interviews helped me gain a clear understanding of my users and their main frustrations and goals. I was able to derive the percentage of users who would be interested in an event management app. Looking at struggles of these users further inspired me to come up with the best solution to their problems. Features according to the need of users were derived.

Competitor Analysis

After personally using and observing the above apps, I identified the major challenges. After this analysis I also read through many google reviews to realize the most common problems. Following are the insights of competitors analysis:

  • Cluttered UI
  • Excessive noise due to unnecessary content
  • Very specific like only to create event & management is not there
  • Confusing flow

A user journey map (also known as a customer journey map) is a diagram that visually illustrates the user flow through your site, starting with initial contact or discovery, and continuing through the process of engagement into long-term loyalty and advocacy.

Userflow

User flow is the path taken by a prototypical user on a website or app to complete a task. The user flow takes them from their entry point through a set of steps towards a successful outcome and final action, such as completing an event.
